# MemoryLUV
### A LUVWORKS product by RMS Omnimedia

**A personalized orientation display for people living with memory challenges, cognitive change, or extended recovery — built by a family caregiver, for families like yours.**

---

## What it does

MemoryLUV is a self-updating digital display designed for the *person being cared for* — not just the caregiver. It runs in any browser on any screen, and gives your loved one something most caregiving tools forget: a way to know what's happening in their day without having to ask.

At a glance, the display shows:

- **Current time** — large, clear, always updating
- **Full date** — day of week, month, date, and year, always visible
- **Live weather** — pulls automatically from your zip code every 30 minutes
- **Today's caregiver** — the right name shows automatically based on the day; switches to the night caregiver at 7 PM
- **Today's schedule** — upcoming appointments with full-screen pop-up reminders and a gentle chime at start time
- **Medication reminders** — full-screen pop-ups with chime at your configured times
- **Water and movement prompts** — automatic hourly and every-2-hour reminders during the day
- **Holidays and special days** — built-in recognition of major US holidays with color accents
- **Day / Evening / Night modes** — the display automatically shifts to a dimmer, warmer palette in the evening and a dark low-glow mode overnight, so it doesn't disturb sleep

---

## The settings panel

The downloaded display file includes a **built-in settings panel** — no code required, ever. Tap the small ⚙ button in the bottom-left corner of the display at any time to:

- Update caregiver names by day
- Change medication times
- Add or edit upcoming appointments
- Switch between **Regular** and **Large Print** text sizes

Changes take effect instantly and are saved automatically to the device.

---

## Text size options

MemoryLUV includes two text size modes for the schedule and reminder sections:

| Mode | Section title | Reminder text |
|---|---|---|
| Regular | 42px | 36px |
| Large Print | 45px | 55px |

Large Print mode was designed specifically for people who wear glasses or are reading from across a room. Toggle it anytime from the settings panel.

## How to set it up

**First time:**
1. Complete the 5-step configurator and click **Download MemoryLUV**
2. Save the `.html` file anywhere on your computer
3. Double-click it to open in Chrome or Edge
4. Press **F11** (PC) or **⌘ Ctrl F** (Mac) for full screen
5. Cast or mirror to your TV, monitor, or tablet — it runs and updates automatically 24/7

**Display options:**
- A 28–32" monitor or TV works beautifully at a distance
- An old iPad or tablet propped on a nightstand works for closer viewing
- You can open it from Dropbox, a USB drive, or any computer — it only needs a browser and an internet connection for live weather

**To update caregivers, medications, or appointments:**
1. Tap the ⚙ button in the bottom-left corner
2. Make your changes in the settings panel
3. Tap **Save & Update Display** — done

## Why this exists

Most caregiving tools are built for caregivers. MemoryLUV is built for the person being cared for.

When someone is living with memory challenges or recovering from illness, small moments of disorientation — not knowing what day it is, who's with them, what's happening next — can compound into anxiety, confusion, and loss of dignity. MemoryLUV gives them a quiet, always-there reference that they can look up and check on their own terms.

The person doesn't need to ask. They can just look.

This product grew out of one family caregiver's experience building exactly this kind of display for her mother — a year of hospital stays, rehab, and coming home to a room with no way to track the day. What started as a personal solution became something other families deserve to have too.
